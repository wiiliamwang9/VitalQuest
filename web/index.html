Embark on Your Fitness Quest

Welcome to VitalQuest – where fitness meets blockchain gaming. Connect your wallet, create your character, and start your journey to better health and rewards.

Features

  • Multi-wallet support (MetaMask, etc.)
  • Personalized AI-driven fitness plans (using simple BMI as MVP)
  • Earn points and exchange for tokens
  • On-chain character data management

About VitalQuest

VitalQuest is a GameFi project built on the BNB Chain that combines fitness, blockchain, and gaming. Develop your character by inputting your height and weight, receive a personalized fitness plan, and earn rewards as you progress.
